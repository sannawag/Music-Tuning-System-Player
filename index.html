Create chord sequences with pure Pythagorean tuning

How to use:

Enter chord sequences with format: Fundamental: intervals, duration=multiplier

A4: 1,3,5, duration=2
440: 1,b3,5,b7, duration=1.5
C#3: 1,3,5,7,9,11, duration=4

Fundamentals: Note names (A4, C#3, Bb5) or frequencies in Hz (440, 261.63)
Intervals: 1, b2, 2, b3, 3, 4, #4/b5, 5, b6, 6, b7, 7, 8 (and compound intervals 9, 10, 11, etc.)
Duration: Multiplier of base duration (optional, defaults to 1)
